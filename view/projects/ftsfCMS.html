Content Management System

For the Fair Trade Software Foundation, I worked with different Scrum teams to create a custom CMS. I have worked with different people on the different custom API's to get data from the database. For example, I get the prices and sizes for different products from de database to display them on the Front-end. I also worked with MySQL Workbench to get relations inside the database, so I know what kind of data I can get.

Besides the different API's that I have built I also worked in the Frond -end. I was responsible for creating different components in Vue.js with Veutify, SCSS and HTML.
